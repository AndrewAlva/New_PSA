The best of our fields, transformed into valuable products.

Our agave comes from different and very carefully selected regions, ensuring that the weather, soil and altitude are propitious for their optimal development, all this in order to get the raw materials that would allow us, since the beginning of our process, to ensure the quality of our products.
PSA group has its own plantations which supply the production facilities, as well as qualified personnel that deal with the field operations, such as: planting, maintenance and harvesting.
